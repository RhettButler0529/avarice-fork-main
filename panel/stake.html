Page FAQ

What is staking?
stakes will accrue daily BNB dividends and LAVA bonus tokens based on the amount of LAVA tokens staked. At the end of every day a daily BNB pool will be calculated and it will be shared and allocate to all the open stakes which will be available for user to withdraw when the stake ends.
What is the BNB pool?
94% of all BNB that enters the daily auction lobby is pooled to be distributed back out to the stakers which we call it the dividends pool.
How is the dividends pool calculated?
dividend pool is split over a period of time based on the contracts day, For example, assuming day 3 ended with a 100 Lobby BNB entry, that BNB will be divided by 3 as it's day 3, and split out over 3 days in chunks of 33.3 BNB. Another example; day 10 ends with 100 BNB, these BNB are split into chunks of 10 which are sent out over 10 days. This caps at day 60
What are bonus tokens?
Users receive bonus LAVA tokens on their active stakes which is highly based on the duration of their stakes. Longer the duration higher the bonus percentage.
Can I cancel my stake?
There is no feature in contract to cancel a stake after you started it however you can sell your stakes at the Buy&Sell section.
When will I receive my dividends ?
After the stake days completed you will be able to end your stake and collect your BNB and LAVA tokens.
