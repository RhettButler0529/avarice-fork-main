Page FAQ

What are auction lobbies?
Auction lobbies are Daily Auctions that offer LAVA tokens for BNB based on the LAVA and BNB pool at the end of the day.
How much LAVA do lobbies pay?
Lobbies LAVA pool will start from 3 million LAVA per day and everyday will be reduced by 0.5% of the previous day's lobby.
What is the average BNB/LAVA convert rate?
The rate/price of the token will depend on how much BNB has been contributed to the lobby on that day. If you watch closely you will be able to spot light days and take advantage by increasing your share of that day's pool.
Where does all the BNB go?
Dividends and buyback! except 5% reserve for promotion and platform development costs.
